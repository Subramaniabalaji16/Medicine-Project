Amlodipine

Amlodipine is a calcium channel blocker used to treat high blood pressure and chest pain. It helps relax blood vessels, making it easier for the heart to pump and lowering blood pressure, thereby reducing the risk of strokes and heart attacks.

Atenolol

Atenolol is a beta-blocker that slows the heart rate and reduces the heart’s workload. It's used to treat high blood pressure, angina, and in some cases, heart rhythm disorders. It helps prevent future heart-related problems.

Losartan

Losartan is an angiotensin receptor blocker (ARB) used to manage high blood pressure and protect the kidneys in people with diabetes. It helps relax blood vessels and lower blood pressure, making it easier for the heart to work efficiently.

Ramipril

Ramipril is an ACE inhibitor that helps lower blood pressure and improve heart function after a heart attack. It works by relaxing blood vessels and reducing strain on the heart. It's commonly prescribed for heart failure prevention.

Metoprolol

Metoprolol is a beta-blocker that treats high blood pressure, angina, and heart rhythm disorders. It slows the heartbeat and reduces blood pressure, helping the heart pump more effectively. It’s also used after heart attacks to prevent complications.
