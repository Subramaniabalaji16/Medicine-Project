Betadine (Povidone Iodine)

Betadine is an antiseptic used for wound cleansing and infection prevention. It kills bacteria, viruses, and fungi. Often used before surgery or on minor cuts and burns, it helps keep the wound area clean and promotes healing.

Soframycin

Soframycin is a topical antibiotic used to treat skin infections, cuts, and burns. It helps prevent bacterial growth and accelerates healing. It's commonly found in first-aid kits and is safe for external use under medical advice.
