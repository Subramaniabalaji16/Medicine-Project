Amoxicillin

Amoxicillin is a broad-spectrum penicillin antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections such as throat, ear, urinary tract, and respiratory infections. It works by stopping the growth of bacteria.

Azithromycin (Zithromax)

Azithromycin is a macrolide antibiotic used to treat infections like sinusitis, bronchitis, pneumonia, and STDs. It works by interfering with bacterial protein synthesis, thus stopping bacterial growth.

Ciprofloxacin

Ciprofloxacin is a fluoroquinolone antibiotic effective against a variety of bacterial infections, including urinary, digestive, and respiratory infections. It should be used with care due to possible side effects on tendons and nerves.

Doxycycline

Doxycycline is a tetracycline antibiotic used to treat infections like acne, malaria, Lyme disease, and respiratory infections. It inhibits bacterial growth by preventing protein production in bacteria.

Cefixime

Cefixime is a third-generation cephalosporin antibiotic that treats infections of the lungs, throat, ears, and urinary tract. It's effective against both Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria.
