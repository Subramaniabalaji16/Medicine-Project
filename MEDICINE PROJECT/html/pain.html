Ibuprofen (Brufen, Advil)

Ibuprofen is an NSAID used to reduce fever and treat inflammation. Ideal for menstrual cramps, arthritis, and toothaches. It blocks prostaglandins ‚Äî chemicals that cause inflammation. Best taken with food to prevent stomach issues.

Aspirin

Aspirin relieves pain, fever, and inflammation and is also used in small doses to prevent heart attacks and strokes. It inhibits platelets to reduce blood clots but may irritate the stomach if not taken carefully.

Diclofenac

Diclofenac treats pain and inflammatory conditions like arthritis. It inhibits enzymes responsible for producing prostaglandins. Available orally and topically. Long-term use may affect the stomach or liver, so use with care.

Naproxen

Naproxen is an NSAID offering long-lasting pain relief for arthritis, menstrual cramps, and muscle aches. It blocks inflammation hormones and is effective for chronic pain, but may cause digestive issues if misused.
